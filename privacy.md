# Privacy Policy

**Boolean Query Generator** is committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

This extension does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

## Local Storage

The extension stores the following data **locally** in your browser using Chrome's storage API:

- **Presets**: Your saved search configurations
- **History**: Your recent query generations

This data never leaves your browser and is only accessible to you.

## Permissions

The extension requires these permissions:

- **storage**: To save your presets and history locally
- **clipboardWrite**: To copy queries to your clipboard when you click "Copy"

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
 
## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.
 
## Contact
 
If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/carlopezzuto/boolean-query-generator).

## Changes

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted in this document.
